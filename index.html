Personal Statement

I am a sophomore undergraduate majoring in Intelligent Science and Technology at the School of Automation at South China University of Technology, a Project 985 (top 25) university in China. My scholarly concerns are mostly focused on the field of Computer Vision within Artificial Intelligence, and I am actively involved in research on Gait Recognition under the guidance of Professor Wenxiong Kang. Up to now, my GPA is 3.75/4.

It was not until I entered university that I became aware of the work related to Artificial Intelligence. As one of the first users of ChatGPT 3.5, I was astounded by the powerful capabilities of AI and desired to understand the principles behind it, which sparked a significant interest in machine learning.

Consequently, I began studying "Dive Into Deep Learning" by Professor Mu Li, as well as portions of CS231n by Professor Fei-Fei Li and CS229 by Professor Andrew Ng. By chance, I joined the university's robotics club as a technical officer, where I completed a visual task involving the identification of dishes in the cafeteria. During the summer, I participated in a reinforcement learning competition hosted by Tencent (competing against both undergraduates and PhD students), and despite not finishing due to a scheduling conflict with my courses, I achieved a ranking in the top 25% in the preliminaries. Later, in my sophomore year, feeling the limited resources of the club, I joined Professor Wenxiong Kang's lab through a course instructor's recommendation to research Gait Recognition.

Before delving into the field of Computer Vision, I found the course setup at school to be overly rigid and the content outdated. In a system unique to China, where advancement is solely based on course grade ranking, everyone studied merely to obtain a better GPA, competing for future jobs with higher salaries. Few were genuinely interested in the marvels behind natural sciences or aspired to benefit humanity. They hardly pondered over their future development or how to realize their life's value, merely drifting along without anyone to discuss Lengend of Galactic Heroes, leaving me feeling oppressed and lonely.

However, after engaging with the field of Computer Vision, I became fascinated with its ability to map reality onto the virtual, astonished by the lifelike images generated by Midjourney, and stunned by Sora's shattering of the belief in seeing is believing. I was also amazed by Apple Vision's ability to build bridges between reality and the virtual world, feeling like a creator of worlds. Realizing the extensive applications of computer vision in robotics and the internet, and knowing there are still many areas requiring human creativity and imagination, my teacher told me, "Images are the world's most information-rich medium, and we have much to accomplish." At the same time, the company established by my family specializes in electrical equipment testing services, including visual inspection, which surprisingly intersects with the field of computer vision. Therefore, I also dream of one day applying this knowledge to enhance the company's operations, expand its influence, and create more social value.Therefore, I decided to delve deeper into the field of Computer Vision.

Envisioning the future, I aim to enter the industry to solve practical problems. Noticing that master's students often lack the time to complete work of high quality, I have decided to apply for a PhD to undertake such tasks. Furthermore, I strongly oppose the practice of mass-producing papers for the sake of publishing, as I refuse to engage in repetitive and meaningless research. I aspire to contribute to meaningful work and genuine advancements, rather than pursuing titles and accolades alone. n addition, I believe that addressing practical issues, including project management, fundraising, and collaboration with others, is also crucial, not just academic prowess. I understand the journey ahead will be challenging, but I aim to become a contributor to pushing the boundaries of human knowledge, which will bring me true fulfillment and a sense of purpose, ensuring that my life is not spent in vain.Therefore, I hope to study in overseas laboratories, enriching my personal experience, honing my rigorous research skills, and meeting like-minded peers to pursue dreams beyond the horizon.
