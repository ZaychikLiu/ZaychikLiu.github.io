Personal Statement

I am a sophomore undergraduate majoring in Intelligent Science and Technology at the School of Automation at South China University of Technology, a Project 985 (top 25) university in China. My scholarly interests primarily lie in the field of Computer Vision within Artificial Intelligence, and I am actively engaged in research on Gait Recognition under the mentorship of Professor Wenxiong Kang. Currently, my GPA stands at 3.75/4.

My fascination with Artificial Intelligence was sparked upon entering university, particularly through my early interaction with ChatGPT 3.5, which showcased the remarkable capabilities of AI. This prompted me to delve deeper into the principles underlying machine learning. I embarked on self-study, immersing myself in resources such as "Dive Into Deep Learning" by Professor Mu Li, alongside segments of CS231n by Professor Fei-Fei Li and CS229 by Professor Andrew Ng.

Beyond theoretical study, I actively sought practical applications, joining the university's robotics club as a technical officer. Here, I successfully completed a visual task involving cafeteria dish identification. Additionally, I participated in a reinforcement learning competition hosted by Tencent during the summer, achieving a ranking in the top 25% despite being unable to finish due to scheduling conflicts with my courses.(compete with Phd students)

Feeling the constraints of limited resources within the robotics club, I sought new avenues for research and growth. Upon recommendation from a course instructor, I joined Professor Wenxiong Kang's lab to delve into Gait Recognition.

Prior to immersing myself in Computer Vision, I found the course setup at school to be overly rigid and the content outdated. In a system unique to China, where advancement is solely based on course grade ranking, everyone studied merely to obtain a better GPA, competing for future jobs with higher salaries. Few were genuinely interested in the marvels behind natural sciences or aspired to benefit humanity. They hardly pondered over their future development or how to realize their life's value, merely drifting along without anyone to discuss how to change the world, leaving me feeling oppressed and lonely. However, the discovery of Computer Vision's capacity to bridge reality with the virtual world, evidenced by projects like Midjourney and Sora, ignited a newfound sense of wonder and creativity within me. The intellectual stimulation derived from studying related research reignited my passion for learning and ignited ambitious aspirations.

Recognizing the extensive applications of computer vision in various industries, including robotics and the internet, and acknowledging the convergence with my family's business in electrical equipment testing services, I aspire to apply this knowledge to enhance operations, expand influence, and create social value.

Looking ahead, my goal is to enter the industry to address practical challenges. Observing the time constraints faced by master's students, I aim to pursue a PhD to undertake high-quality work. However, I am resolute in my stance against the proliferation of superficial research merely for the sake of publication. My ambition is rooted in contributing to meaningful advancements, rather than pursuing titles or accolades alone.

Furthermore, I recognize the importance of practical skills such as project management, fundraising, and collaboration. I am prepared to face the challenges ahead with determination, aspiring to become a contributor to the advancement of human knowledge.

With a vision of studying in overseas laboratories to enrich my experience, refine my research skills, and collaborate with like-minded peers, I am committed to pursuing dreams beyond conventional boundaries.
